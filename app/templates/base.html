Terms and Conditions
Admission Requirements

Applicants must be at least 18 years old and have basic computer literacy skills. No prior programming experience is required for beginner programs.

Payment Policy

Full payment is due before the program start date unless an installment plan is approved. Installment plans require a 30% deposit.

Refund Policy

Cancellations made at least 14 days before the program start date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 14 days will receive a 50% refund. No refunds after the program starts.

Attendance Policy

Students must maintain at least 80% attendance to be eligible for the certificate of completion.

Code of Conduct

All students are expected to adhere to our professional code of conduct. Disruptive behaviour may result in dismissal without refund.
